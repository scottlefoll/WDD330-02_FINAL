Hick's Law - The time it takes to make a decision increases with the number and complexity of choices.

Pocket Penguins

https://www.pocketpenguins.com/

This site is simple and minimalist. It offers offer very few choices to the user at each level of interaction and does not clutter up those choices with lots of graphics and visual distraction.

Fitt's Law - The time to click a target is a function of the distance and size of the target.

Instapaper

https://www.instapaper.com/

All navigation buttons on the Instapaper site are large and central, making navigation decisions quick and intuitive - it's a great example of Fitt's Law.

White Space Design - ‘Whitespace is like air; it is necessary for design to breathe.' Wojciech Zielinski

Ray Charles Video Museum

https://raycharles.cydstumpel.nl/

This site is visually stunning and elegant. Its dramatic use of white space is a counterpoint to the thematic emphasis of the site, and provides great emphasis.
